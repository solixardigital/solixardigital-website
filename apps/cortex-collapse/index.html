The Year is 2187

Megacorporations have replaced nation-states, governing humanity from towering arcology megastructures. The Nexus AI has fragmented into warring factions, and you're a freelance operative caught in the chaos.

🕹

One-Thumb Controls

Auto-attack combat with player-controlled movement. Play anywhere - commute, break time, or couch gaming.

⏱

Variable Sessions

Quick Hack (5 min), Standard Run (15 min), or Deep Dive (30 min). Match your playtime to your schedule.

🧠

Cybernetic Upgrades

Permanent augmentations between runs. Chrome arms, neural implants, and combat enhancements.

🏢

Corporate Factions

Align with megacorps for exclusive weapons and abilities. Your choices shape the story.

🚀

Weapon Evolution

25+ weapons with evolution paths. Combine abilities for devastating power spikes.

📖

Episodic Seasons

New story chapters every 6-8 weeks. Uncover the truth behind the AI uprising.

Track Your Prospecting

Log test pan results with color counts and pan sizes. Record mining sessions with gold weight and calculate efficiency ratings. Keep detailed notes and photos for every location you visit.

BLM Mining Claims Layer

View active and closed BLM mining claims directly on the map. See claim names, types, and filing dates. Know exactly where you can and cannot prospect before you head out.

Historical Mines Data

Discover over 80,000 historical mine locations from USGS data. Filter by commodity to find promising areas. Research where gold has been found before to guide your prospecting.

Powerful Analytics

Track total gold recovered, hours worked, and gold-per-hour efficiency. See estimated value at current spot prices. Understand your most productive locations and techniques.

Live Gold Prices

Real-time gold spot prices with quick reference values for weighing small finds. Know exactly what your gold is worth at any moment. Track price trends over time.

Stay Organized

Filter markers by source (yours, shared, public) and category. Search and sort to find any location quickly. Keep your prospecting data organized and accessible.

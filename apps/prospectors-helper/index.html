Prospector's Helper

The ultimate companion app for gold prospectors and metal detectorists.

About the App

Prospector's Helper is designed for recreational gold prospectors, metal detectorists, and rockhounds. Whether you're panning for gold, hunting for relics, or searching for gemstones, this app helps you:

  • Track Locations - Mark and save your prospecting spots with GPS coordinates
  • Log Finds - Record panning results, gold weights, and equipment used
  • View Reference Data - See BLM mining claims and historical mine locations
  • Share with Friends - Connect with fellow prospectors and share your best spots
  • Analyze Results - View statistics and track your gold recovery over time
  • Work Offline - Full functionality even without cell service
